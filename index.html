Admission Details

  • Classes: Nursery to XII
  • Streams: Arts, Science
  • Admission Criteria: Merit-based, First-come-first-served
  • Affiliation: CBSE (Central Board of Secondary Education)
  • Pay the admission fee RS. 1040

Academic Programs

  • CBSE curriculum with emphasis on STEM education
  • Languages (English, Bengali), Mathematics, Science, Social Science, Computer Science
  • Special Programs: Remedial classes for weak students, enrichment programs for gifted students

Extra-Curricular Activities

  • Sports: Cricket, Football, Basketball, Volleyball, Table Tennis
  • Music and Dance: Vocal and instrumental music, classical and contemporary dance
  • Art and Craft: Painting, drawing, sculpture, pottery
  • Clubs: Debate, quiz, drama, science, robotics

Benefits of Joining Vidyajyoti School

  • Experienced and Qualified Faculty: Our teachers are highly qualified and experienced in their subjects.
  • Small Class Sizes: We maintain a small student-to-teacher ratio to ensure personalized attention.
  • Holistic Development: We focus on developing our students' academic, emotional, and social skills.
  • Extracurricular Activities: We offer a range of extracurricular activities to help our students develop new skills and interests.
  • State-of-the-Art Infrastructure: Our school is equipped with modern facilities, including smart classrooms, laboratories, and sports facilities.
  • Safe and Secure Campus: We prioritize the safety and security of our students, with CCTV surveillance and a secure campus.
  • Regular Parent-Teacher Meetings: We keep our parents informed about their child's progress through regular parent-teacher meetings.
  • Online Portal: We provide an online portal for fee payment, result tracking, and other important updates.
  • Scholarships and Awards: We offer merit-based scholarships and awards for outstanding achievements in academics, sports, and extracurricular activities.
